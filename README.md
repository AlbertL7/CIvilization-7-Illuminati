 # Illuminati Civilization - Civilization VII Mod

  *"The world is governed by very different personages from what is imagined by those who are not behind the scenes."*

  Step into the shadows and lead a civilization built on secrecy, influence, and hidden power. The Illuminati don't conquer through brute force‚Äîthey control through information, gold, and carefully placed agents.

  ---

  ## Features

  ### Civilization Traits
  - **Early Espionage** ‚Äî Gain access to espionage mechanics before other civilizations
  - **Gold Theft Mastery** ‚Äî Enhanced gold theft from rival civilizations
  - **Shadow Network** ‚Äî Unique influence system that spreads your reach across the map
  - **Master of Deception** ‚Äî Reduced detection chance for all covert operations
  - **Deflection** ‚Äî Redirect penalties and suspicion away from your operations

  ### Unique Units

  **Knights Templar** (Replaces Horseman)
  Elite heavy cavalry sworn to protect the Order's secrets. These warrior-monks strike with righteous fury and unwavering loyalty. Available in two tiers as you advance through the ages.

  | Tier | Combat Strength | Movement | Production Cost |
  |------|-----------------|----------|-----------------|
  | I    | 30              | 4        | 100             |
  | II   | 40              | 4        | 140             |

  **Assassins** (Replaces Scout)
  Silent operatives trained in stealth and subterfuge. They move unseen through enemy territory, gathering intelligence and eliminating threats before vanishing into the shadows.

  | Tier | Combat Strength | Movement | Production Cost |
  |------|-----------------|----------|-----------------|
  | I    | 17              | 2        | 70              |
  | II   | 24              | 3        | 100             |

  *Special Abilities: Stealth movement, Ambush bonuses, Diplomatic manipulation*

  ### Unique Wonder

  **Eye of Providence**
  The all-seeing symbol of the Order. A monument to hidden knowledge and unseen power. Grants influence over rival civilizations, enhances your espionage network, reveals hidden information across the map, and provides space for Great Works.

  | Attribute | Value |
  |-----------|-------|
  | Cost | 350 Production |
  | Culture | +2 |
  | Gold | +2 |
  | Diplomacy | +3 |
  | Age | Antiquity |
  | Limit | 1 per World |

  ### Traditions

  Progress through the ranks of the Illuminati by unlocking powerful traditions:

  | Tradition | Unlock | Bonuses |
  |-----------|--------|---------|
  | **Veil of Secrecy** | The Initiation | +2 Culture on Gold Buildings |
  | **Web of Influence** | The Hidden Hand | +2 Influence and +2 Gold per Trade Route |
  | **Forbidden Knowledge** | The Arcanum | +1 Science from Specialists, +3 Science in Towns |
  | **Ascension** | The Grand Design | +1 Gold and +1 Influence per Resource, +5 Combat Strength in enemy territory |

  ### Compatible Leaders
  - **Hatshepsut** ‚Äî The pharaoh who ruled from the shadows
  - **Harriet Tubman** ‚Äî Master of secret networks and covert operations

  ## Installation

  ### Prerequisites

  You may need to install the **Civ Mods Manager** to enable mod support for Civilization VII. Download it from the official site:

  ### Step 1: Download the Mod

  - Click the green **Code** button at the top of this repository
  - Select **Download ZIP**
  - Extract the ZIP file to a location of your choice

  Alternatively, clone the repository:
  ```bash
  git clone https://github.com/AlbertL7/CIvilization-7-Illuminati.git

  Step 2: Locate Your Civ VII Mods Folder

  Navigate to your Civilization VII installation folder:
  C:\Users\<Your UserName>\AppData\Local\Firaxis Games\Sid Meier's Civilization VII\Mods

  Can't find it?
  - The AppData folder is hidden by default. In File Explorer, click View ‚Üí Show ‚Üí Hidden items
  - Alternatively, press Win + R, type %localappdata% and press Enter, then navigate to Firaxis Games\Sid Meier's Civilization VII
  - Or simply search for Firaxis Games in File Explorer

  No Mods folder?
  If the Mods folder doesn't exist, create it manually.

  Step 3: Install the Mod

  Important: Place the illuminati_civ folder directly into the Mods folder.

  Copy the illuminati_civ folder (the one containing Illuminati.modinfo) into your Mods folder.

  Your folder structure should look exactly like this:

  Step 4: Enable the Mod

  1. Launch Civilization VII
