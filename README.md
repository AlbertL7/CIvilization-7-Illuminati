# Illuminati Civilization

A custom Antiquity Age civilization mod for **Sid Meier's Civilization VII**.

> *"A secret society of immense power and influence, the Illuminati operate from the shadows. Keepers of ancient relics such as the legendary Ark of the Covenant."*

## Features

- **Full Antiquity Age Civilization** with unique playstyle focused on Gold and Influence
- **Unique Culture Tree** - Illuminati Traditions with 4 civic nodes
- **3 Unique Unit Lines** - Knights Templar cavalry and Assassin/Sicarii infantry
- **Unique Building** - Secret Lodge (Ageless, can be built in Towns)
- **Unique Quarter** - Shadow Sanctum
- **Unique Wonder** - Eye of Providence

---

## Civilization Ability: Keepers of the Ark

The Illuminati wield ancient power from the shadows.

| Bonus | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Shadow Treasury** | +3 Gold in all Cities |
| **Shadow Network** | +2 Influence in all Cities |

*Note: These bonuses apply to Cities only, not Towns.*

## Unique Culture Tree: Illuminati Traditions

A diamond-shaped progression tree with 4 civic nodes:

```
           The Initiation (150)
                  |
         +-------+-------+
         |               |
  The Hidden Hand    The Arcanum
      (250)            (250)
         |               |
         +-------+-------+
                  |
         The Grand Design (400)
```

### Civic Nodes

| Civic | Cost | Unlocks |
|-------|------|---------|
| **The Initiation** | 150 | Secret Lodge, Veil of Secrecy tradition, +1 Culture in all cities |
| **The Hidden Hand** | 250 | Web of Influence tradition, +2 Gold in all cities, +1 Influence in all cities |
| **The Arcanum** | 250 | Forbidden Knowledge tradition, +2 Science in all cities, +2 Science in Towns |
| **The Grand Design** | 400 | Eye of Providence wonder, Ascension tradition, +1 Tradition Slot, +10% Gold purchase efficiency |

### Traditions

| Tradition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Veil of Secrecy** | +2 Culture on Gold buildings |
| **Web of Influence** | +2 Influence per Trade Route, +2 Gold per Trade Route |
| **Forbidden Knowledge** | +1 Science from Specialists, +3 Science in Towns |
| **Ascension** | +1 Gold per Resource, +1 Influence per Resource |

## Unique Units

### Knights Templar (Heavy Cavalry Line)

Elite mounted warriors and guardians of sacred relics.

| Tier | Replaces | Combat | Cost | Unlocked By |
|------|----------|--------|------|-------------|
| Tier 2 | Chariot | 30 | 100 | Wheel |
| Tier 3 | Horseman | 40 | 140 | Iron Working |

**Abilities:**
- **Oathbound**: +3 Combat Strength in friendly territory
- **Holy Order**: -50% Maintenance cost

### Assassin / Sicarii (Infantry Line)

Covert operatives who strike from the shadows, disguised as common migrants.

| Unit | Tier | Replaces | Combat | Cost | Unlocked By |
|------|------|----------|--------|------|-------------|
| Assassin | 1 | Warrior | 17 | 50 | Game Start |
| Assassin | 2 | Spearman | 24 | 80 | Bronze Working |
| Sicarii | 3 | Phalanx | 32 | 120 | Iron Working |

**Abilities:**

| Ability | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 (Sicarii) |
|---------|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Swift** (+1 Movement) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **First Strike** (+5 CS at full HP) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Shadow Step** (Ignores ZoC) | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Flanking** (+3/+4 CS) | - | +3 | +4 |
| **Poison** (+3 CS vs damaged) | - | - | Yes |

## Installation

1. Download the mod
2. Extract to: `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Firaxis Games\Sid Meier's Civilization VII\Mods\`
3. Enable the mod in-game

### Dependencies

This mod requires:
- **Base Standard** (base game)
- **[Izica's Unit Portrait Visual Remaps](https://forums.civfanatics.com/resources/addon-unit-portrait-visual-remaps.32186/)** - For unit portrait display
